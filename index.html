Hello, dashboard!

Introduction

Dashboard is a UI framework for developing dashboard projects on the web.It's free, flexible, and responsible.

Theme colors

There are 8 theme colors in dashboard: primary, secondary, success, danger, warning, tips, info and dark.Theme colors are used for ui-kits such as buttons, panels and labels.

Theme colors can be customized by overwriting.

Buttons

There are 3 types of buttons: flat, ghost and 3d.
